IntelliStock: Intelligent Demand Forecasting & Inventory Optimization Platform

Machine Learning Python Data Visualization Supply Chain Analytics Ensemble Models

Executive Summary

Developed a supply chain analytics system that uses ensemble machine learning models to optimize inventory management decisions. IntelliStock provides accurate demand forecasting and determines optimal inventory levels to minimize carrying costs while preventing stockouts.

Key Achievements

Advanced Forecasting

Achieved 9.2% mean absolute percentage error (MAPE) in demand forecasts using ensemble machine learning models, significantly outperforming industry average of 15-20%.

Cost Optimization

Reduced inventory carrying costs by 15% through optimized economic order quantities (EOQ) and safety stock calculations tuned to service level targets.

Stockout Prevention

Determined optimal reorder points to minimize stockouts while balancing inventory holding costs, resulting in significant decrease in stockout events.

Technical Implementation

Demand Forecasting Engine

  • Ensemble Machine Learning Approach Combined ARIMA time series modeling, XGBoost regression, and Prophet forecasting with ensemble weighting based on historical accuracy
  • Feature Engineering Leveraged seasonal patterns, promotions, and market factors to improve forecast accuracy
  • Automated Retraining Pipeline Implemented continuous model improvement through automated retraining on new data

Inventory Optimization

  • Economic Order Quantity (EOQ) Calculator Incorporated carrying costs, ordering costs, demand variability, and lead time analysis into a comprehensive optimizer
  • Dynamic Safety Stock Calculated optimal safety stock levels based on service level targets and demand uncertainty
  • Reorder Point Optimization Balanced stockout risk against holding costs through sophisticated modeling

Interactive Analytics Dashboard

  • Real-time KPI Monitoring Tracked key inventory performance metrics through dynamic, interactive visualizations
  • Scenario Planning Built tools for what-if analysis of demand shocks and supply chain disruptions
  • Comparative Analysis Visualized forecast accuracy and supply chain performance metrics over time
