Project Overview

Designed and developed a dynamic Power BI dashboard to identify key drivers of customer churn for a telecom provider with 6,687 users. The goal was to provide actionable intelligence to reduce a high churn rate of 26.86% through demographic, behavioral, and service-based segmentation.

This project was initiated to address the critical issue of customer attrition in the telecom industry, where even small reductions in churn rate can significantly impact revenue and growth. By leveraging data-driven insights, we aimed to create a strategic framework for targeted retention initiatives.

Key Insight: Nearly 60%+ of churn is due to competitors and customer service quality — making these the most critical intervention areas.

Business Impact & Results

Action Area Insight/Change Suggested Expected Impact
Long-Term Contracts Incentivize 1–2 year contracts to reduce churn from 46% to <15% Potential 2x retention in high-risk segment
Customer Onboarding (0–6 months) Targeted engagement during early account tenure Reduce early churn by up to 25%
Unlimited Data Plans Address overuse & unmet expectations via revised plans Prevent ~30% of premium user churn
Service Quality Train support staff; reduce service calls per user Improve NPS & drop churn due to "Attitude"
Geo-Specific Campaigns State-wise churn & service call variation tracked Focused retention efforts per region

Strategic Value

  • Enabled real-time churn monitoring across all demographics and usage patterns.
  • Provided C-suite-ready dashboard to guide pricing, bundling, and support policies.
  • Created data-backed playbook to counteract competitor switching.
  • Identified high-value retention opportunities by targeting specific contract types and service issues.
  • Developed a framework for ongoing analysis and continuous improvement in customer retention strategies.
