Sales Analytics Dashboard (Power BI)

Comprehensive Power BI sales analytics platform featuring 5 interactive dashboards providing deep insights into revenue performance, customer behavior, and market trends. Features advanced visualizations, predictive forecasting, and automated reporting capabilities that drive strategic business decisions and improve sales performance.

Duration: 4 months development and implementation
Role: Senior Data Analyst & BI Developer
Client: Multi-Channel Retail Organization
Live Demo

Dashboard Gallery

Project Overview

This comprehensive Power BI sales analytics solution delivers actionable business intelligence through 5 specialized dashboards designed to optimize sales performance and strategic decision-making. The platform transforms complex sales data into intuitive visualizations and automated insights that empower sales teams, management, and executives to drive revenue growth and operational efficiency.

Business Challenge

The organization faced challenges with fragmented sales data across multiple systems, limited visibility into sales performance trends, and time-consuming manual reporting processes. Sales teams needed real-time insights to identify opportunities, track performance against targets, and make data-driven decisions to improve revenue outcomes.

Power BI Solution Architecture

I designed and developed a comprehensive Power BI solution featuring 5 interconnected dashboards that provide 360-degree visibility into sales operations. The solution integrates data from multiple sources including CRM, ERP, and external market data to deliver real-time insights and automated reporting capabilities.

Dashboard Portfolio (5 Specialized Dashboards)

1. Revenue Performance Dashboard

Executive-level overview of revenue trends, YoY growth, and performance against targets with predictive forecasting.

2. Customer Behavior Analytics

Deep dive into customer segmentation, purchasing patterns, lifetime value analysis, and retention metrics.

3. Product Performance Analysis

Comprehensive product analytics including top performers, margin analysis, and inventory optimization insights.

4. Market Trends & Territory Analysis

Geographic sales performance, market penetration analysis, and territory optimization recommendations.

5. Sales Team Performance Metrics

Individual and team performance tracking, quota attainment, pipeline analysis, and commission calculations.
